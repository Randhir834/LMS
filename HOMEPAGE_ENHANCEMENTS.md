## 🎨 Design Improvements

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Gradients**: Blue to teal, purple to pink, green to yellow
- **Consistent Branding**: PlayFit color palette maintained throughout
- **Accent Colors**: Strategic use of yellow, orange, pink, purple, teal

### Typography
- **Hierarchy**: Clear heading sizes (text-4xl to text-5xl for main headers)
- **Font Weights**: Black (900) for headers, bold (700) for subheadings, medium (500) for body
- **Line Heights**: Relaxed leading for better readability

### Spacing & Layout
- **Generous Padding**: py-28 for major sections (112px vertical padding)
- **Max-Width Containers**: 7xl (1280px) for content sections, 4xl (896px) for text-heavy sections
- **Grid Systems**: Responsive grids (1, 2, 3, or 4 columns based on screen size)

### Animations & Interactions
- **Hover Effects**: Scale (105-110%), translate-y, rotate transforms
- **Gradient Animations**: Smooth color transitions on hover
- **Shadow Transitions**: From subtle to prominent on hover
- **Duration Classes**: 300ms to 700ms for smooth animations

### Icons
- **Lucide React Icons**: Consistent icon library throughout
- **Size Variations**: w-4 to w-8 (16px to 32px)
- **Gradient Backgrounds**: Icons placed in gradient circular/rounded containers
- **Contextual Usage**: Icons match section themes
