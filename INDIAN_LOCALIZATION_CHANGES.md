## 🎯 Cultural Sensitivity Achieved

### **Names Selection Criteria:**
1. **Commonly used** - Names are familiar to Indian audience
2. **Pan-Indian appeal** - Not region-specific slang
3. **Modern yet traditional** - Balance contemporary and classic
4. **Gender appropriate** - Maintains diversity
5. **Professional context** - Suitable for educational platform

### **Regional Representation:**
- **Sharma** - Common in North India
- **Patel** - Common in Gujarat/West India  
- **Reddy** - Common in South India (Telugu-speaking regions)
- **Kumar** - Pan-Indian, used across all regions
