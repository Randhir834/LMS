## 🎨 Design Characteristics

### Colors Used:
- **Primary**: Blue gradients (#1E88E5, #1976D2)
- **Secondary**: Green gradients (#7CB342, #689F38)
- **Accents**: Yellow, Orange, Pink, Purple, Teal
- **Neutral**: Dark navy, white, grays

### Typography Scale:
- **Mega Headers**: text-5xl to text-7xl (48px - 72px)
- **Section Headers**: text-4xl to text-5xl (36px - 48px)
- **Subheadings**: text-2xl to text-3xl (24px - 30px)
- **Body Text**: text-lg to text-xl (18px - 20px)
- **Small Text**: text-sm to text-base (14px - 16px)

### Spacing Pattern:
- **Section Padding**: py-28 (112px top/bottom)
- **Card Padding**: p-8 (32px all around)
- **Gap Between Items**: gap-8 (32px)
- **Max Width**: max-w-7xl (1280px)

### Animation Types:
- **Hover Scale**: 105% - 110%
- **Hover Translate**: -translate-y-1 to -translate-y-3
- **Hover Rotate**: rotate-6
- **Transition Duration**: 300ms - 700ms
- **Shadows**: from lg to 2xl on hover
